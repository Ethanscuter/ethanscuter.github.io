I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at University of Hamburg (Universität Hamburg), advised by Prof. Chris Biemann. Previously, I was a visiting student at SIAT, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018, advised by Prof. Min Yang. Then, I was a research assistant at PolyU, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University from 2018 to 2019, advised by Prof. Maggie Wenjie Li. I received my M.Eng. degree and B.Eng. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2019 and 2016, respectively.

Research

I have broad interests in natural language processing (NLP), particularly in crossmodal learning and natural language generation.

  • Efficient-training in multimodal representation and crossmodal retrieval
  • Cognitive-inspired multimodal representation and reasoning
