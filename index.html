Research

I have broad interests in natural language processing (NLP), particularly in crossmodal learning and natural language generation. My research is mostly driven by two goals:

  • Unsupervised learning in crossmodal learning.
  • Multimodal learning under multilingual context.

Recently, I am dedicated to investigate how multimodal cues improving human reading from both computer science and cognitive prospectives.
